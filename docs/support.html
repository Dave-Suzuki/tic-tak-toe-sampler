Tic Tac Tone is a music-making game where every move on the tic-tac-toe grid activates an instrument. Two players take turns claiming pads — each move adds a new layer to the beat.

FAQ

How do I play?
Tap any pad on the 3×3 grid. Players alternate turns (X and O). Each claimed pad activates an instrument that loops in the sequencer. Get three in a row to win!
How do I change the music style?
Tap the genre buttons at the top (Hip-Hop, Techno, Lo-Fi, etc.). Swipe or tap the arrows to see more genres on page 2.
What do the knobs do?
BPM controls the tempo. Swing adds shuffle. Filter sweeps the sound. Feel adds humanized timing. The XY pads control reverb, delay, distortion, and tremolo.
No sound is playing?
Make sure your device is not on silent mode (check the side switch on iPhone). Try tapping a pad — audio starts on the first tap.
The app requires iOS 17 or later.
Tic Tac Tone requires iOS 17.0 or later. Please update your device if you're on an older version.

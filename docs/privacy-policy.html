Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 28, 2026

Overview

Tic Tac Toe Sampler ("the App") is a music-making game that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is important to us, and the App is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed
  • No data is sent to any server
  • No third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are used
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No advertisements are displayed

Local Storage

The App stores a small amount of data locally on your device (such as your preferred game mode) using standard browser storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

Network Access

The App does not require or use an internet connection. All functionality is self-contained within the app.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the App's support page.

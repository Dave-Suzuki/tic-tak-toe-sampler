#

Tic Tac Tone

Play a game, make a beat

Every move on the 3×3 grid activates a synthesized instrument — kick, snare, bass, keys, pads, and more. Two players take turns claiming pads, and each move adds a new layer to a looping beat. Get three in a row to win.

Features

🎵

8 Genres

Hip-Hop, Techno, Lo-Fi, Ambient, Chiptune, Jungle, House, and Ragga — each with unique instruments and vibe.

🎚

Real-time FX

XY pads for reverb, delay, distortion, and tremolo. Shape your sound while you play.

✎

Pattern Editor

Edit 16-step sequences for any instrument. Note carousel for melodic pads. Undo and auto-save.

🎮

4 Game Modes

2-Player, vs AI, JAM (free sampler), and EDIT (pattern editor). Something for every session.

🔒

No Ads, No Tracking

100% offline. No accounts, no data collection, no internet required. Just music and strategy.

⏱

Tap Tempo

Match any BPM by tapping. Plus Swing for groove and Feel for humanized micro-timing.

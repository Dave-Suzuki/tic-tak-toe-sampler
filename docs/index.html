Features

Everything you need to make a beat

01 — Genres

8 Music Styles

Hip-Hop, Techno, Lo-Fi, Ambient, Chiptune, Jungle, House, and Ragga. Each with its own instruments, tempo, and color scheme.

02 — Effects

Real-time FX

Two XY pads for reverb, delay, distortion, and tremolo. Shape the sound while you play.

03 — Editor

Pattern Editor

Edit 16-step sequences for any instrument. Note carousel for melodic pads. Undo and auto-save.

04 — Modes

4 Game Modes

Two-player, vs AI, JAM for free exploration, and EDIT for custom patterns.

05 — Privacy

No Ads, No Tracking

No accounts, no data collection, no internet required. Just music and strategy.

06 — Timing

Tap Tempo & Swing

Match any BPM by tapping. Add shuffle with Swing. Humanize with Feel.

How It Works

Three moves to a track

01

Tap a pad

Each cell on the 3×3 grid is a different instrument. Claim it by tapping.

02

Build the beat

Every move adds a new layer to a looping sequence. The beat grows as the game progresses.

03

Win the break

Get three in a row and your winning instruments play a solo break before the next round.
